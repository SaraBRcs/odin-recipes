Arroz al Horno

What is it?

Arroz al horno, in English oven-baked rice, is a dish full of flavour that also looks great on the plate. The ingredients vary, but generally it always contains cured meats, pork ribs and pancetta, as well as tomatoes, garlic, chickpeas and, of course, Valencia Designation of Origin rice.

Back in the day, the name of this dish was "arròs passejat" in Valencian language, meaning “gone-for-a-walk rice” in English, as once the first steps of the recipe were done, locals would take their traditional terracotta dishes full to the brim to the nearest communal oven or bakery as not everybody had a home oven back then. Imagine the delectable aromas that would have filled the streets in those days.

Ingredients (4 servings)

  • 200g of Valencia Designation of Origin rice
  • 600mL of Cocido Style Broth
  • 250g of cooked chickpeas
  • 2 thick slices of pork belly
  • 250g pork ribs
  • 2 blood sausages
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 potato
  • 1 head of garlic
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Slice, brown and set aside the potatoes

    Slice the potatoes into rounds, then brown them in a paella pan with extra virgin olive oil. Once done, set them aside.

  2. Sauté the pork belly slices and blood sausages

    In the same paella pan, add the pork belly slices and the blood sausages. Sauté them until they are golden brown.

  3. Add the rice and cooked chickpeas

    Next, add the rice and mix it well with the ingredients already in the paella pan. Add the cooked chickpeas.

  4. Pour in the broth

    Pour the cocido broth over the mixture before adding the remaining ingredients.

  5. Add the remaining ingredients

    Arrange the remaining ingredients, such as the browned potato rounds, the sliced tomato, and the unpeeled head of garlic on top. Add salt to taste.

  6. Cooking time

    Place the paella pan in a preheated oven at 250°C and bake for 19 minutes. If you notice it needs more broth during cooking, you can add a little more.

  7. Ready to serve

    Remove from the oven and serve immediately while hot.
