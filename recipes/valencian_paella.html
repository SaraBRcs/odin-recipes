Valencian Paella

What is it?

Valencian Paella is the symbol and most internationally renowned dish of Valencia. It is the perfect way to gather friends and family together, and cooking it is a ritual. The guests usually stay in the kitchen and chat with the cook while they wait until everything is ready.

The main ingredientes and method are always the same, though there can be some slight variations depending on the season and the cook's personal preferences.

Ingredients

The most popular ingredients for Valencian Paella are the following:

  • 500g of Valencia Designation of Origin rice.
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • 1 chopped rabbit
  • 1/2 chopped free-range chicken
